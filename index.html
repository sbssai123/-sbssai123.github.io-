Hi, I'm Sreeya.

I am a senior at Northeastern University studying computer science with an interest in conversational AI, venture capital, and entrepreneurship. Through Northeastern's co-op program, I have received one year of industry experience. My first software engineering co-op was at IBM Research on the Healthcare Effectiveness Research Team working on understanding and analyzing physician-patient conversations. After woking at a large corporate organization, I wanted to explore Boston's tech start-up scene so I worked at Catalant Technologies for my second co-op.

I am also the Chief Business Officer for TechTogether (formerly SheHacks), a non-profit organization focused on equipping young women with the tools and resources they need to explore the tech industry. Last year, our organization held SheHacks Boston, one of the largest all-female and non-binary hackathons in the world where I served as the Director of Partnerships and a founding team member. I am also a Venture Fellow at Rough Draft Ventures, a student VC firm supported by General Catalyst, and make investment decisions to back student founded start-ups.

After graduating in May 2019, I will be working full-time at Drift in Boston as a Software Engineer.
